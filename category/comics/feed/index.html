Despite my aforementioned general clumsiness and penchant for falling down all over the place, I actually do exercise pretty often. Mostly it’s indoors things with weights and cardio or Pilates. But about a month ago, I started running twice or three times a week, building up each day on my times and speed and then, one night, my knees just start aching a lot. And since I have no insurance, I had to self-diagnose with the help of the super trusty Yahoo! Answers (-_-) and I think it’s Runner’s Knee. Bah! It’s painful enough to keep me up at nights when it does happen, but mysteriously it feels fine when I run. But still, continued lack of healthcare means I should probably just keep away from the running for now. *sigh*

So here we are! I shan’t be making excuses for lack of updates, merely I’ll explain what’s happening in my life thus far. It’s been nothing exciting, nothing especially comic worthy in any case, but it’s been hard work. For the past couple of months, I’ve been working as hard as I can updating my portfolio and demo reel and working on new projects and burning through as many tutorials and books as I can get my hands on and my mind around. The job-hunt-of-sorts has really been consuming almost everything I have. Every time I take a break, I berate myself for not going down the list of things that I need to do: why haven’t I written that new cover letter? Why haven’t I started that tutorial on nHair driven joints in Maya? Why haven’t I perfected the shading group of my character’s render in mental ray on the background I made? My resume is a month old, why haven’t I updated the format?

Yeah. I don’t think this will ever be done until I get a job, and I feel guilty working on things that won’t eventually get into the portfolio. So until then, which for my mental sake I hope is soon, expect to see sporadic updates as in the past couple of months on GU.

Thanks as always for reading!

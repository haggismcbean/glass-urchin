An Odd Stereotype

So, looking up Zodiac on wiki to see how to spell the plural form of “Virgo” (no luck there), I ended up reading the entire article on the Zodiac Killer. Creepy stuff! Wikipedia’s somewhat personal and highly factual accounts of the killings and investigation is almost like reading an abridged version of a Capote-esque take on the case.

Interestingly, (and showing the amazing tenacity of those Wiki-ites out there) the article had been updated to say that a man in Sacremento, CA, found several items he claimed belonged to his now deceased grandfather. He turned these items, including a bloodied knife, rolls of film of a gruesome nature, and a black hood with the Zodiac Killer’s sign on it, over to the police on September 1st, 2008 (just three days before this post)! …And so the plot (finally) thickens… I guess I’ll be keeping an eye on the wiki page for updates. (Back to looking up cheerful things on wiki, like Grover, Gargoyles comics, and Nothlits)

So yea. Zodiac and stuff! I’m a Virgo. How about you?

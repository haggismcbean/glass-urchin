My dad doesn’t really talk to me too much. We do talk, but not usually things regarding emotion or opinion, so I tend to think that dad and I might not be as close as we should be. I do remember being much closer when I was younger, but I’m not sure if anything really happened to make us distanced. Anyway, maybe that’s just the way he is.

This story is one he recalled to me not too long ago, about when I was little (and had to follow dad into the men’s room all the time). See! Even back then I wanted to be a boy! I guess though, I was just surprised that dad would recall a story about me like that with clarity- that he shared a chuckle with a stranger over me wanting to use a urinal. It seemed like he even had the dialogue down pat – the accent by the way is an attempt to portray my dad’s way of speaking… He was born and raised in France and while it is a French accent, it’s also a little weird.

I think this might just be a story about how I want a penis.

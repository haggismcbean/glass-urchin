It’s more of the idea – that warmth that drives people to enjoy life outdoors and spend time together, before they have to go hole up at home to weather the cold times. Anyway it’s just a nightmare, as stated, leaves haven’t turned just yet…

In terms of alone – it’s just last year was my first time in a long time without a steady partner, and this year it’s my first time in a long time being away from close family and friends.

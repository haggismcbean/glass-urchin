Hey all

So sorry that I couldn’t finish this bout of comics before I left for my vacation. When you read this, I’ll be boarding a plane to go to Zurich, then to Athens, then a ferry to a very tiny island with some of my classmates. I wanted to finish this series (just one more page left…) before I left, before my birthday this week, but alas…. Work, coupled with moving, packing, and saying goodbye to all the people who helped be through this year… Got in the way.

I’ll be returning to Edinburgh in two weeks time, but until then I’ve sworn myself off of computers for that short while as a respite from what we had to go through for our dissertations.

Again, many apologies. In GU’s long run, I’ve been proud to never have missed an update for you… But rather than completely rush through some bum excuse comics, I’ll just begin again when I’m back home in Embra.

Thanks again for your patience and support, everyone! I am lucky to have readers like you!

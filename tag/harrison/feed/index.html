Oh I am such a dork. I know Heart Gold/Soul Silver are just remakes of a series that has basically stayed the same since the beginning, but I just love starting out a new Pokemon journey. Gets me everytime! I don’t know if it’s the collecting aspect or the building-up your team aspect, but it’s just one of those ones that I’ll play anytime (but screw those other offshoot Pokemon games, they’re lame).

I think we all have something we need to dork out about. I’m not just saying it HAS to be about dorky things – it can be anything from your love of Coruscant architecture to letterboxing to Final Fanasty potions to baseball… Everyone has something they know and love really well. And I think if you hit upon the subject with people, it might come blasting out like a gusher.

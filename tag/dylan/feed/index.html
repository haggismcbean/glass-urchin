Just use an iPhone.

I’ve never been a fan of Apple products. I like to look at them and their advertising, and I’m sure I would love to have some if they weren’t so expensive (I did receive an iTouch once, and I promptly sold it because I was in dire need of money). But the iPhone is one of the few Apple products that actually look like it’s worth it to have, rather than a “would be nice” category that most iPods fall under. If I got one though, I think I might go crazy with my compulsive e-mail and website state checking that I might never be able to leave the net ever again. I need not tell you what those things can do!

In other words, all that iPhone power would probably get to my head, and I might destroy the world. So for now, I remain a mere mortal – people like Dylan and Heather can use and abuse the power while people like Bryan and myself watch on, amazed.

Out Up and Down

Taping, gluing, and jamming together those pieces, bit by bit. Some semblance of normality coming… soon I hope?

Not to say I don’t like going out, I enjoy it and my friends very very much! Just to say that… I’m still waiting for everything to “go away” as they all say.

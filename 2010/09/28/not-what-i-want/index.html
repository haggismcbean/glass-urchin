Not What I Want

Sorry that I’ve been depressing lately guys. It just that some things are hard to forget. Things have, for the most part, faded and softened but one phrase continues to cut me deep. I think about… everything that is wrong and undesirable in me. Am I what anyone wants? Am I what the right one wants?

Don’t worry about me guys, I’ll be fine, honest!

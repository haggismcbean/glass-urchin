The Nine to Five Job

Good ol’ Sisyphus, your frequently referenced-to-day-jobs existence just is a little off for me! But then again, the Prince sure makes rolling katamari around look so easy… And working in the Toy Industry is not so, especially if you work for a smaller company. I guess it could be like when you try to roll a giant katamari up a giant slope… What does your work’s (or school’s or self-employed activities) daily items look like?

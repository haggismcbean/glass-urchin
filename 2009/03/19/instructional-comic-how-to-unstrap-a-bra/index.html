Instructional Comic: How to Unstrap a Bra

So you guys asked for it, here it is! The first of hopefully more instructional comics.

Ever feel trapped by clasps? We know it’s Mike’s weakness. Now, with this easy tutorial, you can learn how to unclasp the restrictive and complex bra and free the boobs within! Overcome your weakness, today!

edit – Come to think of it, how would you describe taking off a bra? A bunch of people have apparently found my page by googling “how to unstrap a bra,” so I just went with that. But I guess it could also be “unlatch a bra,” “unhook a bra,” or “unclasp a bra”…. Or more simply, taking a bra off.

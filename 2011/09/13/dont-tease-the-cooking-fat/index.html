Don’t Tease the Cooking Fat

When do I not live with cats? I guess it’s just destiny. Triskal and Boris are kind of like more extremely annoying, older, and crankier versions of the kitties back home – Triskal is more conniving and troublesome but a bit like Zoe, and Boris is needy like Ally but to an overly extreme degree. And Zoe talks all the time too, at least it’s a cute meow! Boris literally sounds as though someone is throttling him.

By the way, do you know British rhyming slang? It’s confusing and weird, but it’s certainly interesting.

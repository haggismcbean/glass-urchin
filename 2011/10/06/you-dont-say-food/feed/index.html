]]> By: Sophie H http://glassurchin.nerdcomics.com/2011/10/06/you-dont-say-food/comment-page-1/#comment-9057 Sophie H Sun, 09 Oct 2011 11:29:23 +0000 http://glassurchin.nerdcomics.com/?p=1703#comment-9057 On the topic of lemonade, I thought I’d bring in an Australian opinion for added confusion:
lemonade is any clear soft drink (soda water is not lemonade, 7up is a lemonade)
lemon squash is any (usually yellow) lemon-flavoured soft drink (mountain dew is kind of a lemon squash, it’s got lime in it too though)
The American style stuff is more like a cordial, but we still call it lemonade.

We also call both fries and crisps “chips”, for added confusion.
